## Nordic Regulatory Bodies & Contexts

### Denmark
- **Data Protection:** Datatilsynet (Danish Data Protection Agency)
- **Cybersecurity:** Center for Cybersikkerhed (CFCS)
- **Certification:** DANAK (Danish Accreditation)
- **ISO 27001 Context:** Strong focus on GDPR compliance, financial sector regulation (Finanstilsynet)
- **NIS2 Implementation:** Focus on critical infrastructure (energy, transport, healthcare)

### Finland
- **Data Protection:** Tietosuojavaltuutetun toimisto (Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman)
- **Cybersecurity:** TRAFICOM (Finnish Transport and Communications Agency Cybersecurity Centre)
- **Certification:** FINAS (Finnish Accreditation Service)
- **ISO 27001 Context:** High-tech sector focus, strong telecommunications security
- **NIS2 Implementation:** Emphasis on digital service providers, telecommunications

### Norway
- **Data Protection:** Datatilsynet (Norwegian Data Protection Authority)
- **Cybersecurity:** Nasjonal sikkerhetsmyndighet (NSM - Norwegian National Security Authority)
- **Certification:** Norsk Akkreditering (Norwegian Accreditation)
- **ISO 27001 Context:** Oil & gas sector security, maritime cybersecurity
- **NIS2 Implementation:** Critical infrastructure (energy, maritime, healthcare)

## Professional Translator Guidance

### Required Expertise
1. **ISO 27001:2022 Knowledge:** Professional information security certification background
2. **Nordic Language Fluency:** Native or near-native proficiency in Danish, Finnish, or Norwegian
3. **Technical Writing:** Experience with cybersecurity documentation
4. **Cultural Adaptation:** Ability to preserve Discordian humor while adapting for Nordic markets
5. **Regulatory Knowledge:** Familiarity with GDPR, NIS2, and Nordic cybersecurity landscape

### Translation Style Guide
- **Tone:** Professional but irreverent, questioning authority while maintaining ISO 27001 accuracy
- **Voice:** Preserve Hagbard Celine and Simon Moon narrative voices from Illuminatus! trilogy
- **Technical Terms:** Keep English for: AWS, GitHub, YubiKey, OWASP, MITRE ATT&CK, STRIDE
- **Frameworks:** Keep English for: ISO 27001, NIST CSF, CIS Controls, GDPR, NIS2
- **Humor:** Adapt for Nordic sensibilities - Danes/Norwegians appreciate dry wit, Finns prefer directness
- **Philosophy:** "Think for yourself, question authority" should resonate with Nordic democratic values

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
1. ❌ Don't translate ISMS-PUBLIC repository links
2. ❌ Don't change ISO 27001 Annex A control numbers (A.5.1, A.6.2, etc.)
3. ❌ Don't localize technical product names (AWS, GitHub, Slack, etc.)
4. ❌ Don't remove Discordian references (FNORD, Eris, Chapel Perilous)
5. ❌ Don't over-formalize - maintain irreverent tone appropriate to Discordian philosophy
6. ❌ Don't translate URLs or email addresses
7. ❌ Don't change Schema.org structured data keys
