🏛️ Monitor del Parlamento de la UE

AI-Powered Parlamento Europeo Intelligence

AI-powered Parlamento Europeo Intelligence Platform with 8 unified gh-aw workflows + 1 translate, producing autonomous political news in 14 languages. 51-artifact análisis baseline, 17 methodologies, 52 templates, 19 tradecraft references — 170+ daily runs generating 1,700+ artifacts. 🎯 Características Principales

🤖 8 Unified Agentic Flujos de Trabajo

Fully autonomous news generation with 8 unified gh-aw workflows + 1 translate. AI workflows produce deep análisis político including daily summaries, perfiles de eurodiputados, party analyses, committee reports — zero human editors in the loop.

📊 51-Artifact Análisis Baseline

Integral análisis baseline with 17 methodologies and 52 templates. Structured OSINT tradecraft with 19 tradecraft references for intelligence-grade análisis político.

⚡ 170+ Daily Runs

Alto-frequency autonomous operation generating 1,700+ artifacts. Continuous monitoreo of Parlamento Europeo activities with real-time news production across 14 languages.

🔌 62 Herramientas MCP Integration

Powered by the Servidor MCP del Parlamento Europeo with 62 tools (15 inteligencia OSINT + 47 acceso a datos). MEP puntuación de influencia, análisis de coaliciones, anomaly detection.

🌍 14 Language Support

Full multi-language AI news generation supporting 14 languages (English, Swedish, Arabic, Hebrew RTL, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and 7 European languages) for global democratic transparency.

📄 SEO-Optimized Output

Structured news output with SEO optimization, schema.org markup, and accessibility compliance. Published directly to static site with automated deployment.

⚙️ Capacidades de la Plataforma

👥 MEP Profiles

Integral profiles for all eurodiputados including voting history, party affiliation, national delegation, membresía en comisiones, and activity metrics.

🏛️ Political Group Análisis

Political group-level statistics and cross-group voting análisis. Rastrea group cohesion, voting discipline, and coalition patterns across EU legislative issues.

📋 Legislative Seguimiento

Follow EU legislative proposals through the full legislative process. Rastrea first reading, second reading, amendments, committee reports, and final votes with completo history.

🔎 Advanced Buscar

Powerful búsqueda across Parlamento de la UE documents, votes, debates, and perfiles de eurodiputados. Filter by topic, date, committee, grupo político, or national delegation.

📊 Voting Analytics

Detallado análisis of all Parlamento Europeo votes including party alignment seguimiento, individual patrones de votación, and cross-party análisis de coaliciones.

🌍 Multi-Language Support

Acceso Parlamento de la UE data across all official EU languages. The platform supports the multilingual nature of the Parlamento Europeo's work and documentación.
