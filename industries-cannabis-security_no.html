Why Cannabis Businesses Need Strong Cybersecurity

The legal cannabis industry faces unique operational challenges that make robust cybersecurity essential:

  • High-Value Operations: Cannabis businesses handle valuable inventory, cash transactions, and sensitive customer data‚Äîmaking them attractive targets for cybercriminals
  • Complex Payment Systems: Banking restrictions require alternative payment solutions, increasing the need for secure payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Strict Regulatory Environment: Heavy oversight requires robust data tracking, audit trails, and compliance documentation
  • Patient Privacy (Medical Cannabis): Medical cannabis operations handle sensitive health information requiring strong data protection
  • Supply Chain Complexity: Track-and-trace systems, IoT sensors, and inventory management create multiple attack surfaces
