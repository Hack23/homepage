Resumen del Proyecto

The Monitor del Parlamento de la UE is a integral open-source platform for monitoreo the Parlamento Europeo's activities with systematic transparency. It tracks eurodiputados, sesiones plenarias, committees, legislative documents, and registros de votación using official Parlamento Europeo open data. The platform is fully ISMS-compliant with ISO 27001:2022, NIST CSF 2.0, CIS Controls v8.1, and GDPR.

Características Principales

MEP Monitoreo

Current and historical MEP information including voting history, membresía en comisiones, attendance, and actividad parlamentaria.

Plenary Seguimiento

Integral seguimiento of sesiones plenarias including votes, attendance, speeches, and debates.

Committee Monitoreo

Monitor committee meetings, documents, rapporteurs, and membership across all Parlamento Europeo committees.

Document Buscar

Buscar and acceso textos legislativos, reports, amendments, and official Parlamento de la UE documents.

Preguntas Parlamentarias

Rastrea preguntas parlamentarias and institutional answers. Monitor MEP accountability actions.

MCP Server Integration

Integration with Servidor MCP del Parlamento Europeo for AI-powered acceso to EP data via Model Context Protocol.
