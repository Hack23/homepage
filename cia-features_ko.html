Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Citizen Intelligence Agency's 투명성 platform, data sources, and analytical capabilities.

What is Citizen Intelligence Agency?

Citizen Intelligence Agency (CIA) is an open-source intelligence platform for 투명성 in Sweden, monitoring Swedish parliamentary activity with comprehensive data-driven insights.

Key Features:

  • Political Monitoring: Track politician behavior, voting records, and committee work
  • Party Analytics: Monitor political party activities and performance metrics
  • Legislative Tracking: Follow government bills, motions, and policy proposals
  • Transparency Dashboard: Visualize parliamentary activity and accountability metrics
  • Open Data: All data sourced from official Swedish government APIs

Built as a volunteer-driven initiative, it applies advanced data analysis methodologies to democratic oversight and governance evaluation.

What political data does Citizen Intelligence Agency track?

Citizen Intelligence Agency tracks comprehensive Swedish political data from official sources:

Individual Politician Data:

  • Voting records on all parliamentary decisions
  • Speeches and parliamentary questions
  • Committee work and assignments
  • Attendance and participation metrics

Political Party Activities:

  • Party performance and voting patterns
  • Coalition behavior and alliances
  • Policy positions and priorities
  • Election results and campaign data

Legislative & Government Data:

  • Government bills and legislative proposals
  • Committee meetings and decisions
  • Document publications and transparency metrics
  • Budget proposals and financial decisions
  • Minister and government member activities

All data is aggregated from official Swedish government sources including the Riksdag (parliament) and government agencies.

How is the data collected and verified?

Citizen Intelligence Agency maintains data integrity through rigorous sourcing and validation:

Data Sources:

  • Official APIs: Automated collection from Swedish Parliament's open data APIs
  • Government Databases: Direct access to official document repositories
  • Public Records: Authorized government information systems
  • No Manual Entry: Eliminates human bias and entry errors

Validation Process:

  • Data consistency checks across multiple sources
  • Automated validation against official records
  • Community verification through open-source transparency
  • Same data available to journalists and government officials

The open-source nature allows anyone to review data processing methodologies and verify accuracy, ensuring transparency in both the data and the analysis.

Who uses the Citizen Intelligence Agency platform?

The platform serves diverse users interested in Swedish 투명성:

  • Journalists & Media: Research political stories, fact-check claims, analyze voting patterns
  • Researchers & Academics: Study Swedish politics, voting behavior, and democratic processes
  • Engaged Citizens: Monitor elected representatives and hold them accountable
  • Voters: Understand politician track records before elections
  • Civil Society Organizations: Track policy issues and advocacy outcomes
  • Students: Learn about Swedish parliamentary democracy in action
  • International Observers: Study Scandinavian political systems and transparency
  • Political Analysts: Evaluate government performance and opposition strategies

The platform democratizes access to political data that was previously difficult to analyze systematically, enabling data-driven political engagement.

Is the political data updated in real-time?

The platform updates data regularly based on official government publication schedules:

Update Frequency:

  • Parliamentary Votes: Updated within hours to days of official publication
  • Speeches & Documents: Available as published by the Riksdag
  • Committee Meetings: Updated based on official meeting records
  • Government Decisions: Published according to official schedules

Priority & Accuracy:

  • Accuracy prioritized over instantaneous updates
  • All data validated before display
  • Last update timestamps visible for each dataset
  • Sufficient frequency for meaningful political monitoring

While not instantaneous, the update schedule enables effective tracking of ongoing parliamentary activities and political developments.

How do I navigate and use the platform effectively?

Citizen Intelligence Agency offers multiple intuitive navigation paths:

Main Entry Points:

  • Dashboard Overview: High-level political activity metrics and trends
  • Politician Search: Find representatives and view complete activity profiles
  • Political Party Browser: Compare party performance and voting patterns
  • Policy Area Filter: Track specific issues (healthcare, education, environment)
  • Committee View: Monitor committee work and specialized topics
  • Timeline: Chronological view of political developments
  • Document Library: Search government publications and proposals

Advanced Features:

  • Data visualization tools for deeper analysis
  • Export options for researchers and journalists
  • Social media sharing for engagement
  • Customizable alerts for tracked topics

For detailed guidance, see the platform documentation.

Is Citizen Intelligence Agency only for Sweden or available for other countries?

Citizen Intelligence Agency is currently focused exclusively on Swedish politics:

Current Scope:

  • Optimized for Swedish political structures and parliamentary procedures
  • Integrated with Swedish government data APIs and systems
  • Tailored to Riksdag (Swedish Parliament) operations
  • Swedish language support for official documents

Open Source Potential:

  • Adaptable Codebase: Available on GitHub for other countries
  • Methodology Transfer: Technology can be adapted to other democracies
  • Requirements: Similar open data availability and government APIs
  • International Recognition: Featured in studies on parliamentary monitoring

While Nordic country expansion is conceptually possible, it requires significant development and equivalent data access. The platform serves as a model for democratic transparency initiatives worldwide.
