## Translation Standards Research

### Arabic Standards (from Web Research + Translation Guide)

**ISO 27001 Official Terminology:**
- Information Security Management System: نظام إدارة أمن المعلومات
- Confidentiality: السرية
- Integrity: سلامة (التكامل)
- Availability: التوفر (الإتاحة)
- Risk Management: إدارة المخاطر
- Compliance: الامتثال
- Access Control: التحكم في الوصول
- Incident Response: الاستجابة للحوادث

**Best Practices:**
- Use Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) for business content
- Reference international ISO/IEC 27000 family glossaries
- Preserve English for widely-accepted technical terms
- Formal business register for C-level audience
- Cite GCC cybersecurity frameworks where applicable

### Hebrew Standards (from Web Research + Translation Guide v4.0)

**Israeli Tech Industry Practice:**
- Blend Hebrew-English technical terms (common in startups)
- Hebrewize verbs: לדפלוי (deploy), לטסט (test), להדבג (debug)
- Preserve English: DevSecOps, CI/CD, AWS, GitHub, Docker
- Official regulatory terms from Privacy Protection Authority
- Balance startup informality with professional credibility
