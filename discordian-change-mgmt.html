üõ°Ô∏è The Five Change Management Stages

1. Request

Document what's changing.

What changes? Why? When? Risk level? Standard vs emergency? Document before implementing.

2. Assessment

Analyze impact and risk.

What could break? Who's affected? Dependencies? Rollback plan? Assess before approving.

3. Approval

Authority before action.

Who approves? Standard changes pre-approved. High-risk changes need CAB. No approval = no change.

4. Implementation

Execute with oversight.

Test in staging. Communicate to stakeholders. Implement during maintenance window. Monitor closely.

5. Review

Learn from outcomes.

Did it work? What broke? What learned? Post-implementation review improves future changes.
