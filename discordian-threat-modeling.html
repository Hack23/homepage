Know Thy Enemy: Adversary Modeling

Different adversaries, different capabilities, different motivations:

  • Script Kiddies ‚Äî Low skill, automated tools, mass scans. Defend with basics.
  • Cybercriminals ‚Äî Financially motivated, targeted, patient. Ransomware, exfiltration, fraud.
  • Insiders ‚Äî Already have access. Your biggest threat that security teams ignore.
  • Competitors ‚Äî Corporate espionage. They want your IP, your customers, your secrets.
  • Nation-States ‚Äî Infinite resources, zero-days, supply chain access, legal compulsion. If they want in, they're in.

Your threat model should include the worst case‚Äînation-state adversaries‚Äîbecause that sets your defensive baseline. If you can defend against APTs, script kiddies are noise.
