## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Issue:** Poor translation quality
- **Prevention:** Start with sample translation (1 blog, 1 language)
- **Solution:** Have backup translator vetted and ready

**Issue:** Technical terminology inaccurate
- **Prevention:** Provide Translation-Guide.md files
- **Solution:** Engage subject matter expert for review

**Issue:** Cultural adaptation fails
- **Prevention:** Native speaker review required
- **Solution:** Iterate with translator using feedback

**Issue:** Budget overruns
- **Prevention:** Get fixed quotes upfront
- **Solution:** Pause and re-evaluate per language ROI

**Issue:** Timeline delays
- **Prevention:** Set clear milestones with penalties
- **Solution:** Have contingency timeline (+2 weeks buffer)
