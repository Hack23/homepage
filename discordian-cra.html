üõ°Ô∏è The Five CRA Requirements

1. Essential Cybersecurity Requirements

Secure by design, secure by default.

Products must be designed with security from day one. No default passwords. Security updates for product lifetime. Vulnerability handling processes.

2. Conformity Assessment

Prove your product is secure.

Self-assessment for most products. Third-party assessment for critical products (Class I/II). CE marking after conformity demonstrated.

3. Documentation Requirements

Brussels wants paperwork.

Technical documentation, risk assessments, security testing results, vulnerability disclosure policies. EU Declaration of Conformity required.

4. Vulnerability Management

Lifecycle security responsibility.

Monitor for vulnerabilities. Issue security updates. Coordinate disclosure. Notify authorities of actively exploited vulnerabilities within 24 hours.

5. Market Surveillance

Enforcement with teeth.

Fines up to ‚Ç¨15M or 2.5% of global turnover. Products can be recalled from market. Authorities can demand documentation audits.
