Swedish Parliament Intelligence Platform

Riksdagsmonitor is an open-source Swedish Parliament Intelligence Platform that monitors political activity with systematic transparency. Built on the proven Citizen Intelligence Agency (CIA) platform, it provides 50+ years of historical data, real-time analysis, and supports 14 languages, making Swedish parliamentary data accessible to citizens, researchers, and journalists worldwide.
