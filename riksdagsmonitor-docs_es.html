Resumen del Proyecto

Riksdagsmonitor is an open-source Swedish Parliament Intelligence Platform that monitors political activity with systematic transparency. Built on the proven Citizen Intelligence Agency (CIA) platform, it provides 50+ years of historical data, real-time análisis of Riksdag activities, and supports 14 languages. This documentación hub covers the completo arquitectura, seguridad design, testing frameworks, and SGSI compliance documentación.

Características Principales

Parliamentary Monitoreo

Real-time seguimiento of Swedish Riksdag activity including MP profiles, registros de votación, committee work, and interpellations.

50+ Years Historical Data

Integral archive spanning over 50 years of Swedish actividad parlamentaria with trend análisis capabilities.

14 Language Support

Full multi-language platform making Swedish parliamentary acceso a datosible to international audiences.

Political Analytics

Advanced behavior análisis, transparency metrics, and evidence-based seguimiento de la rendición de cuentas.

Data Quality

Automated validation with digital signatures, sourced from official Riksdag open data APIs.

99.998% Disponibilidad Target

Alto availability arquitectura with integral business continuity planning and disaster recovery strategies.
