🔍 Citizen Intelligence Agency

Political Transparency Through Open Data

Open-source intelligence (OSINT) platform for political transparency in Sweden. Monitor parliamentary activity, voting records, and political behavior with comprehensive data-driven insights and accountability metrics.

📂 View on GitHub ✨ Features 📚 Documentation 🗳️ Election 2026

🎯 Key Features

🏛️ Parliamentary Monitoring

Real-time tracking of Swedish Riksdag activity including debates, proposals, committee work, and legislative processes. Comprehensive historical data analysis.

🗳️ Voting Records Analysis

Detailed analysis of all parliamentary votes with party alignment tracking, individual voting patterns, and coalition analysis. Transparency in democratic decision-making.

📊 Political Analytics

Data-driven insights into political behavior, party strategies, and legislative trends. Statistical analysis of political performance and accountability.

📈 Data Visualization

Interactive dashboards and charts for exploring political data. Visual representation of voting patterns, attendance records, and legislative activity.

✅ Accountability Metrics

Track politicians' promises, attendance, voting consistency, and activity levels. Objective measures of political performance and engagement.

🔓 Open Data Integration

Integration with official Swedish government open data sources. Automated updates and real-time synchronization with parliamentary systems.

🎯 Mission & Impact

🌟 Democratic Transparency

Empowering citizens with access to political information and accountability data. Making democracy more transparent and accessible to everyone.

Impact: Increased citizen engagement in democratic processes through better access to political information.

📰 Press Recognition

Featured in Computer Sweden and Riksdag och Departement for innovative approach to political transparency. Recognized by National Democratic Institute.

Impact: Broader awareness of the importance of parliamentary monitoring and civic technology.

🔍 Evidence-Based Politics

Providing objective data for researchers, journalists, and citizens to analyze political behavior and hold representatives accountable.

Impact: More informed public discourse based on facts rather than rhetoric.

⚙️ Platform Capabilities

👤 Politician Profiles

Comprehensive profiles for all Riksdag members including voting history, party affiliation, committee memberships, and activity metrics.

🏛️ Party Analysis

Party-level statistics and coalition analysis. Track party cohesion, voting discipline, and strategic positioning across issues.

📋 Legislative Tracking

Follow bills and proposals through the legislative process. Track amendments, committee reviews, and final votes with full historical context.

🔎 Search & Discovery

Powerful search functionality across debates, votes, and documents. Filter and analyze data by topic, date, politician, or party.

📊 Custom Reports

Generate custom reports for specific time periods, politicians, or issues. Export data for research and analysis.

🔔 Alerts & Notifications

Stay informed about specific politicians, parties, or issues. Automated alerts for voting activity and legislative changes.

🛠️ Technology Stack

☕ Backend

Java, Spring Framework - Enterprise-grade backend with robust data processing, API integration, and business logic.

🖥️ Frontend

Vaadin Framework - Modern web UI framework for rich, responsive user interfaces with server-side architecture.

🗄️ Database

PostgreSQL - Reliable relational database for complex political data relationships and historical records.

🔒 Security

SLSA Level 3 - Supply chain security, secure development practices, and continuous security monitoring.
