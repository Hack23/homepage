Citizen Intelligence Agency::Tracking politicians like bugs.
Objective
Visualize political activity in Sweden, present key performance indicators and metadata for the actors on national level.
Data
Importing open data from http://data.riksdagen.se/ , http://www.val.se/ and http://data.worldbank.org/ .
Coverage
Country indicators, government, government bodies,ministries,government members parliament members, committees, documents, ballots, decisions and political parties.
How
Analyzing the data using Business Rules Management System, Statistical Machine Intelligence and Learning Engine and visualize the result.
