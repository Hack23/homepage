Application
Citizen Intelligence Agency::Tracking politicians like bugs.
Objective
Visualize political activity in Sweden, present key performance indicators and metadata for the actors on national level.
Data
Software agents gather data from public api:s Swedish National Financial Management Authority, Swedish Parliament, Swedish Election and World Bank
Coverage
Country Indicators, Rule Violations, Risk Summary, Metadata, Charts, Government, Government Bodies, Ministries, Government Members, Parliament Members, Committees, Documents, Ballots, Decisions and Political parties.
How
Analyzing the data using Business Rules Management System, Statistical Machine Intelligence and Learning Engine and visualize the information/Intelligence.

Sonar Cloudformation Plugin
Objective
Integrate Security Check of Cloudformation before deployment.
How
