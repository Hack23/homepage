משולש CIA Components

זמינות Components

זמינות ensures information and systems are accessible when needed. Our platform helps you implement appropriate זמינות controls based on your business needs:

  • Basic: Manual backup and recovery with ~95% uptime (5% CAPEX/5% OPEX)
  • Moderate: Pilot light standby systems with ~99% uptime (15% CAPEX/15% OPEX)
  • High: Warm standby with fast recovery and ~99.9% uptime (25% CAPEX/40% OPEX)
  • Very High: Multi-site active/active with real-time failover and ~99.99% uptime (60% CAPEX/70% OPEX)

Each level includes detailed technical implementation guidance, CAPEX/OPEX drivers, and business impact analysis to help you make informed decisions.

שלמות Components

שלמות maintains the accuracy and completeness of data. Our platform helps you implement appropriate controls:

  • Basic: Manual validation with minimal checks (5% CAPEX/10% OPEX)
  • Moderate: אוטומטי validation with enhanced accuracy (20% CAPEX/20% OPEX)
  • High: Blockchain validation with immutable records (35% CAPEX/50% OPEX)
  • Very High: Smart contracts with real-time validation (60% CAPEX/70% OPEX)

Each level includes technical implementation details and business impacts to help you align your שלמות controls with your organization's needs.

סודיות Components

סודיות ensures information is accessible only to authorized users. Our platform helps you implement appropriate controls:

  • Basic: Public data with minimal protection (5% CAPEX/5% OPEX)
  • Moderate: Restricted data with AES-256 encryption (15% CAPEX/20% OPEX)
  • High: Confidential data with MFA and robust monitoring (30% CAPEX/40% OPEX)
  • Very High: Secret data with quantum-safe encryption (50% CAPEX/60% OPEX)

Each level includes technical implementation details tailored to different data sensitivity requirements.

Which ציות frameworks does מנהל ציות CIA support?

מנהל ציות CIA provides automated ציות mapping to multiple major frameworks:

  • NIST: אבטחת סייבר Framework and NIST 800-53 controls
  • ISO 27001: Information Security Management System requirements

The platform automatically maps your security controls to relevant framework requirements, making ציות reporting efficient and comprehensive. This eliminates manual cross-referencing and ensures you don't miss critical requirements.

How does איום modeling work in the platform?

מנהל ציות CIA integrates STRIDE איום modeling methodology to systematically identify and mitigate security threats:

STRIDE Categories:

  • Spoofing: Identity impersonation and authentication bypass threats
  • Tampering: Unauthorized modification of data or systems
  • Repudiation: Denial of actions without audit trail
  • Information Disclosure: Unauthorized access to sensitive data
  • Denial of Service: זמינות disruption and resource exhaustion
  • Elevation of Privilege: Unauthorized access to higher permissions

Platform תכונות:

  • Guided איום identification for systems and data flows
  • Automatic mapping of threats to security controls
  • Risk visualization dashboards showing איום landscape
  • Prioritized mitigation recommendations based on likelihood and impact
  • Integration with ציות requirements for comprehensive coverage
