üîç The Five Questions for Real Compliance

Ask these before claiming compliance:

  1. Can you prove controls work? - Evidence > documentation. Logs, tests, metrics.
  2. Are policies enforced technically? - Don't rely on humans following procedures.
  3. Do you know when controls fail? - Monitoring, alerting, incident detection.
  4. Can you respond to breaches? - Tested incident response, not theoretical plans.
  5. Is compliance continuous? - Not point-in-time for audits‚Äîongoing assurance.
ULTIMATE ILLUMINATION: Compliance frameworks exist because market forces failed to incentivize security. Use frameworks as roadmaps‚Äînot destinations. The goal is security, not certification.
