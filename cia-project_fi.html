🔍 Citizen Intelligence Agency

Poliittinen läpinäkyvyys avoimen datan kautta

Avoin lähdetieto (OSINT) -alusta poliittiseen läpinäkyvyyteen Ruotsissa. Seuraa parlamentaarista toimintaa, äänestyspöytäkirjoja ja poliittista käyttäytymistä kanssa kattavia dataohjautuvia näkemyksiä ja vastuullisuusmittareita.

📂 Katso GitHubissa ✨ Ominaisuudet 📚 Dokumentaatio 🗳️ Election 2026

🎯 Keskeiset ominaisuudet

🏛️ Parlamentaarinen seuranta

Reaaliaikainen seuranta of Swedish Riksdag activity including debates, proposals, committee work, ja legislative processes. Comprehensive historical data analysis.

🗳️ Äänestyspöytäkirjojen analyysi

Yksityiskohtainen analyysi of all parliamentary votes kanssa party alignment tracking, individual voting patterns, ja coalition analysis. Transparency in democratic decision-making.

📊 Poliittinen analytiikka

Dataohjautuvat näkemykset into political behavior, party strategies, ja legislative trends. Statistical analysis of political performance ja accountability.

📈 Datan visualisointi

Interaktiiviset kojelaudat ja charts for exploring political data. Visual representation of voting patterns, attendance records, ja legislative activity.

✅ Vastuullisuusmittarit

Track politicians' promises, attendance, voting consistency, ja activity levels. Objective measures of political performance ja engagement.

🔓 Avoimen datan integraatio

Integration kanssa official Swedish government open data sources. Automated updates ja real-time synchronization kanssa parliamentary systems.

🎯 Missio ja vaikutus

🌟 Demokraattinen läpinäkyvyys

Empowering citizens kanssa access to political information ja accountability data. Making democracy more transparent ja accessible to everyone.

Impact: Increased citizen engagement in democratic processes through better access to political information.

📰 Lehdistötunnustus

Featured in Computer Sweden ja Riksdag och Departement for innovative approach to political transparency. Recognized by National Democratic Institute.

Impact: Broader awareness of the importance of parlamentaarinen seuranta ja civic technology.

🔍 Näyttöön perustuva politiikka

Providing objective data for researchers, journalists, ja citizens to analyze political behavior ja hold representatives accountable.

Impact: More informed public discourse based on facts rather than rhetoric.

⚙️ Alustan ominaisuudet

👤 Poliitikkoprofiilit

Comprehensive profiles for all Riksdag members including voting history, party affiliation, committee memberships, ja activity metrics.

🏛️ Puolueanalyysi

Party-level statistics ja coalition analysis. Track party cohesion, voting discipline, ja strategic positioning across issues.

📋 Legislative Tracking

Follow bills ja proposals through the legislative process. Track amendments, committee reviews, ja final votes kanssa full historical context.

🔎 Search & Discovery

Powerful search functionality across debates, votes, ja documents. Filter ja analyze data by topic, date, politician, or party.

📊 Custom Reports

Generate custom reports for specific time periods, politicians, or issues. Export data for research ja analysis.

🔔 Alerts & Notifications

Stay informed about specific politicians, parties, or issues. Automated alerts for voting activity ja legislative changes.

🛠️ Teknologiapino

☕ Backend

Java, Spring Framework - Enterprise-grade backend kanssa robust data processing, API integration, ja business logic.

🖥️ Frontend

Vaadin Framework - Modern web UI framework for rich, responsive user interfaces kanssa server-side architecture.

🗄️ Database

PostgreSQL - Reliable relational database for complex political data relationships ja historical records.

🔒 Security

SLSA Level 3 - Supply chain security, secure development practices, ja continuous security monitoring.

🛡️ Turvallisuus ja laatu

Citizen Intelligence Agency demonstrates enterprise security practices for handling sensitive political data kanssa integrity ja availability.

🗳️ Ruotsin vaalit 2026

Special focus on the upcoming 2026 Swedish national election kanssa enhanced monitoring ja analysis capabilities.

📊 Election Analytics

Comprehensive analysis of electoral trends, campaign promises, ja political positioning leading up to the 2026 election.

🎯 Accountability Tracking
