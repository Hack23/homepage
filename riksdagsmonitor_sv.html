🏛️ Riksdagsmonitor

AI-driven riksdagsövervakning

Världens första helt autonoma AI-politiska nyhetsredaktion for Riksdagen. 11 agentiska arbetsflöden produce djupgående politisk analys — inte grunda sammanfattningar, utan strukturerad OSINT-underrättelse covering 349 riksdagsledamöter, 2 494 historiska politiker, 3,5M+ röster, and 109 000+ dokument in 14 språk. Noll mänskliga redaktörer krävs.

🎯 Key Features

🤖 11 Agentisk AI Workflows

Fully autonom nyhetsgeneration with 91 skills. AI workflows produce djupgående politisk analys including daily summaries, MP profiles, party analyses, committee reports, and Tidö coalition tracking — zero human editors in the loop.

🗳️ 3.5M+ Vote Records

Comprehensive analysis of 3.5M+ parliamentary votes with 349 current MPs and 2 494 historiska politiker. Party alignment tracking, voting anomaly detection, and 45-rule × 349-MP riskvärmekarta.

📄 109,000+ Documents

Full-text analysis of 109,000+ parliamentary documents including motions, interpellations, committee reports, and legislation. AI-powered summarization and cross-referencing.

🌍 Stöd för 14 språk

Full flerspråkiga AI-nyheter generation supporting 14 språk (Swedish, English, Arabic, Hebrew RTL, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and 7 European languages) for global demokratisk transparens.

📊 45-Rule Riskvärmekarta

Advanced political risk scoring with 45 compliance/behavior rules applied across 349 riksdagsledamöter. Anomaly detection, early warning system, and accountability tracking dashboards.

🏛️ Tidö Coalition Tracker

Specialized monitoring of the Tidö Agreement coalition dynamics, voting cohesion analysis, and policy implementation tracking for Sweden's current government configuration.

🎯 Platform Overview

🌟 Autonomous Politisk underrättelse

Disrupting parliamentary journalism with AI-generated intelligence. What used to require a team of analysts can now be done by any citizen with an AI assistant.

Impact: Democratising access to politisk underrättelse — non-partisan, open-source, architecturally designed for equal treatment of all political groups.

🔄 Built on CIA Data Backbone

Powered by the Citizen Intelligence Agency — 6 analysis frameworks, 50 risk rules, 110 database views (77 politicians + 33 documents), and 4 OSINT sources with OpenSSF Scorecard 7.2/10.

Foundation: Enterprise-grade open-source platform with SLSA Level 3 supply chain security, zero critical CVEs for 5+ years.

🔍 OSINT Tradecraft Applied

Structured open-source intelligence methodology applied to public legislative data. Source verification via official Riksdag APIs, not scraped social media.

Trust: GDPR-by-design, public-data only, no user accounts, no ads, no tracking. Cannot be weaponised for partisan influence.

⚙️ Platform Capabilities

👤 MP Profiles

Comprehensive profiles for all Riksdag members including voting history, party affiliation, committee memberships, interpellations, and activity metrics.

🏛️ Party Analysis

Party-level statistics and coalition analysis. Track party cohesion, voting discipline, and strategic positioning across issues and legislative periods.

📋 Committee Tracking

Follow committee work, proposals, and decisions through the legislative process. Track committee composition, activity levels, and policy influence.

💬 Interpellation Monitoring

Track all interpellations (parliamentary questions) to ministers with full response history. Monitor accountability mechanisms and government responsiveness.

📊 Custom Reports

Generate custom reports for specific time periods, politicians, or issues. Export data for research and analysis purposes.

🔗 Öppna data-integration

Integration with official svenska riksdagen open data sources. Automatiserade uppdateringar och realtidssynkronisering with riksdagssystemen.

🛡️ Security & Quality

Riksdagsmonitor demonstrates enterprise security practices with 99.998% availability target, comprehensive ISMS documentation, and full compliance with ISO 27001:2022, NIST CSF 2.0, and CIS Controls v8.1.
