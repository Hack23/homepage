Parlamento Europeo Intelligence Platform

The Monitor del Parlamento de la UE is an open-source platform focused on political transparency at the European Union level. It provides integral monitoreo of the Parlamento Europeo's activities, enabling citizens, researchers, journalists, and civil society organizations to hold EU representatives accountable through data-driven insights from official Parlamento Europeo open data.

MEP Monitoreo

Member Profiles & Activity Seguimiento

Integral profiles for all current and historical Miembros del Parlamento Europeo. Rastrea voting behavior, membresía en comisiones, actividad legislativa, and attendance records across parliamentary sessions. The platform provides objective, data-driven insights into MEP performance and accountability.

MEP Profile Características:

📊 Voting History

Completo registros de votación for each MEP across all plenary votes. Analizar voting consistency with grupo político positions and cross-party patrones de votación.

📋 Committee Work

Rastrea MEP participation in parliamentary committees, including meetings attended, reports authored, and positions held within committee structures.

📅 Attendance Records

Monitor MEP attendance at sesiones plenarias and committee meetings. Compare attendance rates against peers and historical norms.

🏛️ Political Group Affiliation

Rastrea changes in grupo político membership over time. Analizar the relationship between national party affiliation and European grupo político alignment.

Plenary Session Seguimiento

Integral Plenary Monitoreo

Full monitoreo of Parlamento Europeo sesiones plenarias, capturing all votes, speeches, debates, and official proceedings. The platform provides detallado análisis of EU legislative decisions and parliamentary proceedings from official Parlamento Europeo records.

Plenary Características:

🗳️ Vote Análisis

Detallado results for all plenary votes including individual MEP positions, grupo político alignment, and final outcomes. Rastrea how different groups and nations vote on key EU legislation.

🎤 Debate Monitoreo

Acceso speeches and debate contributions from eurodiputados during sesiones plenarias. Buscar for contributions on specific topics or from specific representatives.

📅 Session Archives

Completo historical archive of Parlamento Europeo sesiones plenarias. Buscar and analizar historical votes and debates to rastrear EU political trends over time.

📊 Attendance Analytics

Session-level attendance data showing which eurodiputados were present for key votes and debates. Identify patterns in participation and absence.

Committee Monitoreo

Parliamentary Committee Intelligence

Integral monitoreo of all Parlamento Europeo committees where much of the detallado legislative work takes place. Rastrea committee meetings, documents, rapporteurs, and membership to understand EU legislative preparation.

Committee Características:

🏛️ Committee Profiles

Detallado information on each parliamentary committee including mandate, membership, leadership, and current legislative agenda. Rastrea the full landscape of EU committee structures.

📋 Meeting Records

Acceso records of committee meetings, agendas, and outcomes. Monitor how committees prepare legislation before it reaches the full Parliament.

📄 Committee Documents

Buscar and acceso committee reports, opinions, and working documents. Rastrea the legislative process from initial committee stage through to final vote.

👤 Rapporteur Seguimiento

Identify and monitor rapporteurs responsible for drafting committee reports. Understand who shapes EU legislation at the committee stage.

Legislative Document Buscar

Full Legislative Document Acceso

Powerful búsqueda and navigation across the completo corpus of Parlamento Europeo legislative documentación. Acceso textos legislativos, committee reports, amendments, opinions, and all official EU documentos parlamentarios through an intuitive interface.

Document Características:

🔍 Full-Text Buscar

Buscar across all Parlamento de la UE documents by keyword, topic, MEP name, committee, or date range. Find relevant legislation and documentación quickly.

📊 Legislative Status

Rastrea the current status of legislative proposals through the EU legislative process. See where each initiative stands from first reading to final adoption.

📝 Amendment Seguimiento

Monitor amendments proposed and adopted during the legislative process. Understand how legislation evolves from initial Commission proposal to final text.

🌍 Multi-Language Acceso

Acceso Parlamento de la UE documents in all official EU languages. Navigate multilingual textos legislativos aligned with the Parlamento Europeo's language policy.

Preguntas Parlamentarias

Questions & Accountability Seguimiento

Rastrea preguntas parlamentarias submitted by eurodiputados to la Comisión Europea, Council, and other EU institutions. Monitor how eurodiputados use the formal questioning procedure to hold EU institutions accountable and advance constituent interests.

Parliamentary Question Características:

❓ Question Archive

Completo archive of preguntas parlamentarias by topic, MEP, grupo político, or target institution. Understand what issues eurodiputados are raising on behalf of their constituents.

📋 Response Seguimiento

Monitor institutional responses to preguntas parlamentarias. Rastrea response times and quality of answers from the Commission and other EU institutions.

📊 Question Analytics

Analizar question patterns by topic, MEP activity, and grupo político focus areas. Identify emerging political priorities through question frequency análisis.

🔎 Topic Análisis

Identify the most active topics in parliamentary questioning. Rastrea which EU policy areas receive the most scrutiny from elected representatives.

AI Acceso via Servidor MCP del Parlamento Europeo

AI-Powered Parlamento de la UE Intelligence

Monitor del Parlamento de la UE integrates with the Servidor MCP del Parlamento Europeo, enabling asistentes de IA to consulta and analizar Parlamento Europeo data programmatically via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). This integration brings EU inteligencia política directly into AI workflows.

MCP Integration Características:

🤖 AI Consultar Interface

asistentes de IA like Claude can consulta Parlamento de la UE data directly through MCP tools. Ask lenguaje natural questions about eurodiputados, votes, committees, and legislation.

🔌 MCP Protocol

Implements the Model Context Protocol standard for AI tool integration. Compatible con any MCP-enabled AI system including Claude, and other major AI platforms.

📡 Real-Time Data

AI consultas acceso current Parlamento Europeo data updated from official EP open data sources. Provides AI systems with accurate, up-to-date EU inteligencia política.

🛡️ Secure Integration

MCP server integration follows seguridad best practices. Read-only acceso to public Parlamento de la UE data ensures data integrity and privacy compliance.
