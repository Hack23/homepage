Common questions about Citizen Intelligence Agency's political transparency platform, data sources, og analytical capabilities.

What is Citizen Intelligence Agency?

Citizen Intelligence Agency (CIA) is an open-source intelligence platform for political transparency in Sweden, monitoring Svensk parlamentarisk activity med comprehensive datadrevne indsigter.

Key Funktioner:

  • Political Overv친gning: Track politician behavior, voting records, og committee work
  • Party Analytics: Monitor political party activities og performance metrics
  • Legislative Tracking: Follow government bills, motions, og policy proposals
  • Transparency Dashboard: Visualize parliamentary activity og ansvarligheds metrics
  • Open Data: All data sourced from official Swedish government APIs

Built as a volunteer-driven initiative, it applies advanced data analysis methodologies to democratic oversight og governance evaluation.

What political data does Citizen Intelligence Agency track?

Citizen Intelligence Agency tracks comprehensive Swedish political data from official sources:

Individual Politician Data:

  • Voting records on all parliamentary decisions
  • Speeches og parliamentary questions
  • Committee work og assignments
  • Attendance og participation metrics

Political Party Activities:

  • Party performance og voting patterns
  • Coalition behavior og alliances
  • Policy positions og priorities
  • Election results og campaign data

Legislative & Government Data:

  • Government bills og legislative proposals
  • Committee meetings og decisions
  • Document publications og transparency metrics
  • Budget proposals og financial decisions
  • Minister og government member activities

All data is aggregated from official Swedish government sources including the Riksdag (parliament) og government agencies.

How is the data collected og verified?

Citizen Intelligence Agency maintains data integrity through rigorous sourcing og validation:

Datakilder:

  • Official APIs: Automated collection from Swedish Parliament's open data APIs
  • Government Databases: Direct access to official document repositories
  • Public Records: Authorized government information systems
  • No Manual Entry: Eliminates human bias og entry errors

Validation Process:

  • Data consistency checks across multiple sources
  • Automated validation against official records
  • Community verification through open-source transparency
  • Same data available to journalists og government officials

The open-source nature allows anyone to review data processing methodologies og verify accuracy, ensuring transparency in both the data og the analysis.

Who uses the Citizen Intelligence Agency platform?

The platform serves diverse users interested in Swedish political transparency:

  • Journalists & Media: Research political stories, fact-check claims, analyze voting patterns
  • Researchers & Academics: Study Swedish politics, voting behavior, og democratic processes
  • Engaged Citizens: Monitor elected representatives og hold them accountable
  • Voters: Understand politician track records before elections
  • Civil Society Organizations: Track policy issues og advocacy outcomes
  • Students: Learn about Svensk parlamentarisk democracy in action
  • International Observers: Study Scandinavian political systems og transparency
  • Political Analysts: Evaluate government performance og opposition strategies

The platform democratizes access to political data that was previously difficult to analyze systematically, enabling data-driven political engagement.

Is the political data updated in real-time?

The platform updates data regularly based on official government publication schedules:

Update Frequency:

  • Parliamentary Votes: Updated within hours to days of official publication
  • Speeches & Documents: Available as published by the Riksdag
  • Committee Meetings: Updated based on official meeting records
  • Government Decisions: Published according to official schedules

Priority & Accuracy:

  • Accuracy prioritized over instantaneous updates
  • All data validated before display
  • Last update timestamps visible for each dataset
  • Sufficient frequency for meaningful political monitoring

While not instantaneous, the update schedule enables effective tracking of ongoing parliamentary activities og political developments.
