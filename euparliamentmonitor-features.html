European Parliament Intelligence Platform

The EU Parliament Monitor is an open-source platform focused on political transparency at the European Union level. It provides comprehensive monitoring of the European Parliament's activities, enabling citizens, researchers, journalists, and civil society organizations to hold EU representatives accountable through data-driven insights from official European Parliament open data.

MEP Monitoring

Member Profiles & Activity Tracking

Comprehensive profiles for all current and historical Members of the European Parliament. Track voting behavior, committee memberships, legislative activity, and attendance records across parliamentary sessions. The platform provides objective, data-driven insights into MEP performance and accountability.

MEP Profile Features:

📊 Voting History

Complete voting records for each MEP across all plenary votes. Analyze voting consistency with political group positions and cross-party voting patterns.

📋 Committee Work

Track MEP participation in parliamentary committees, including meetings attended, reports authored, and positions held within committee structures.

📅 Attendance Records

Monitor MEP attendance at plenary sessions and committee meetings. Compare attendance rates against peers and historical norms.

🏛️ Political Group Affiliation

Track changes in political group membership over time. Analyze the relationship between national party affiliation and European political group alignment.

Plenary Session Tracking

Comprehensive Plenary Monitoring

Full monitoring of European Parliament plenary sessions, capturing all votes, speeches, debates, and official proceedings. The platform provides detailed analysis of EU legislative decisions and parliamentary proceedings from official European Parliament records.

Plenary Features:

🗳️ Vote Analysis

Detailed results for all plenary votes including individual MEP positions, political group alignment, and final outcomes. Track how different groups and nations vote on key EU legislation.

🎤 Debate Monitoring

Access speeches and debate contributions from MEPs during plenary sessions. Search for contributions on specific topics or from specific representatives.

📅 Session Archives

Complete historical archive of European Parliament plenary sessions. Search and analyze historical votes and debates to track EU political trends over time.

📊 Attendance Analytics

Session-level attendance data showing which MEPs were present for key votes and debates. Identify patterns in participation and absence.

Committee Monitoring

Parliamentary Committee Intelligence

Comprehensive monitoring of all European Parliament committees where much of the detailed legislative work takes place. Track committee meetings, documents, rapporteurs, and membership to understand EU legislative preparation.

Committee Features:

🏛️ Committee Profiles

Detailed information on each parliamentary committee including mandate, membership, leadership, and current legislative agenda. Track the full landscape of EU committee structures.

📋 Meeting Records

Access records of committee meetings, agendas, and outcomes. Monitor how committees prepare legislation before it reaches the full Parliament.

📄 Committee Documents

Search and access committee reports, opinions, and working documents. Track the legislative process from initial committee stage through to final vote.

👤 Rapporteur Tracking

Identify and monitor rapporteurs responsible for drafting committee reports. Understand who shapes EU legislation at the committee stage.

Legislative Document Search

Full Legislative Document Access

Powerful search and navigation across the complete corpus of European Parliament legislative documentation. Access legislative texts, committee reports, amendments, opinions, and all official EU parliamentary documents through an intuitive interface.

Document Features:

🔍 Full-Text Search

Search across all EU Parliament documents by keyword, topic, MEP name, committee, or date range. Find relevant legislation and documentation quickly.

📊 Legislative Status

Track the current status of legislative proposals through the EU legislative process. See where each initiative stands from first reading to final adoption.

📝 Amendment Tracking

Monitor amendments proposed and adopted during the legislative process. Understand how legislation evolves from initial Commission proposal to final text.

🌍 Multi-Language Access

Access EU Parliament documents in all official EU languages. Navigate multilingual legislative texts aligned with the European Parliament's language policy.

Parliamentary Questions

Questions & Accountability Tracking

Track parliamentary questions submitted by MEPs to the European Commission, Council, and other EU institutions. Monitor how MEPs use the formal questioning procedure to hold EU institutions accountable and advance constituent interests.

Parliamentary Question Features:

❓ Question Archive

Complete archive of parliamentary questions by topic, MEP, political group, or target institution. Understand what issues MEPs are raising on behalf of their constituents.

📋 Response Tracking

Monitor institutional responses to parliamentary questions. Track response times and quality of answers from the Commission and other EU institutions.

📊 Question Analytics

Analyze question patterns by topic, MEP activity, and political group focus areas. Identify emerging political priorities through question frequency analysis.

🔎 Topic Analysis

Identify the most active topics in parliamentary questioning. Track which EU policy areas receive the most scrutiny from elected representatives.

AI Access via European Parliament MCP Server

AI-Powered EU Parliament Intelligence

EU Parliament Monitor integrates with the European Parliament MCP Server, enabling AI assistants to query and analyze European Parliament data programmatically via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). This integration brings EU political intelligence directly into AI workflows.

MCP Integration Features:

🤖 AI Query Interface

AI assistants like Claude can query EU Parliament data directly through MCP tools. Ask natural language questions about MEPs, votes, committees, and legislation.

🔌 MCP Protocol

Implements the Model Context Protocol standard for AI tool integration. Compatible with any MCP-enabled AI system including Claude, and other major AI platforms.

📡 Real-Time Data

AI queries access current European Parliament data updated from official EP open data sources. Provides AI systems with accurate, up-to-date EU political intelligence.

🛡️ Secure Integration

MCP server integration follows security best practices. Read-only access to public EU Parliament data ensures data integrity and privacy compliance.
