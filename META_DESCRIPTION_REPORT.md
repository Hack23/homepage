## SEO Benefits

### Length Optimization
- **140-160 characters**: Optimal for Google search results (desktop shows ~155-160 chars, mobile shows ~120 chars)
- All descriptions now fit within this sweet spot
- No truncation in search results, maximizing click-through potential

- **Keyword integration**: Natural inclusion of primary keywords (cybersecurity, ISO 27001, ISMS, GDPR, etc.)
