Black Trigram is a revolutionary 2D precision combat simulator inspired by traditional Korean martial arts. It combines authentic martial arts techniques with modern gaming to create an educational and engaging experience.

How does the 70 vital points system work?

The game features 70 anatomically accurate vital points based on traditional Korean martial arts knowledge and modern anatomy:

  • Strategic Targeting: Each vital point has specific effects on an opponent - some cause immediate damage, others impair movement or create openings
  • Anatomical Accuracy: Points are positioned based on real human anatomy, making the system educational as well as tactical
  • Contextual Effectiveness: The effectiveness of targeting specific points depends on combat situation, opponent stance, and timing
  • Skill-Based Gameplay: Rewards anatomical knowledge and precision over button mashing
  • Traditional Knowledge: Based on classical Korean martial arts teachings about pressure points and vital areas

Mastering the vital points system requires understanding human anatomy, timing, and strategic thinking - making combat more tactical and rewarding.
