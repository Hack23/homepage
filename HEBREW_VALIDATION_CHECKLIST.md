### 3. Natural Hebrew Fluency (Weight: 25%)

**Check for:**
- [ ] Sentences flow naturally in Hebrew
- [ ] No "translationese" (word-for-word translation feeling)
- [ ] Proper Hebrew sentence structure
- [ ] Natural word order
- [ ] Idiomatic expressions appropriate to context

**Common Issues to Spot:**
- English sentence structure in Hebrew
- Unnatural word order
- Missing or incorrect Hebrew connectors (×•, ××ª, ×”)
- Awkward passive voice (Hebrew prefers active)
- Incorrect use of definite article (×”)

**Rating Scale:**
- 5 = Reads like native Hebrew content
- 4 = Mostly fluent, minor awkwardness
- 3 = Noticeable translation feeling
- 2 = Frequent awkward constructions
